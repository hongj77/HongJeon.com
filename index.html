Hi, I'm Hong.

Full-stack web developer, Android developer, cheese enthusiast.

I study Computer Science and Mathematics at Cornell University..

Currently in my second year, I have been living by the motto: learn, learn, and learn some more. Viewing every opportunity as a learning opportunity has helped my mind sprout in all directions and discipline. I always try to intake new information without any bias and without any filters. This philosophy has had a tremendous positive effect on the way I live life; I am always eager to learn something new. Teach me something!

Ultimately, I am interested in pursing a career path that involves machine learning, artificial intelligence, robotics, and entrepreneurship.

When I am not performing computer magic, I am a strong advocate for Starcraft 2, Eminem, dysfunctional pickup basketball games, barefoot ultimate frisbee, and the TV show Legend of Korra.

ME THINGS

Flash Animation

Squartlevision is an animation channel with 18k subscribers who want to view badly drawn cartoons. I started this project when I was 15 to join the Youtube Partner program.

ACM Programming Team

Although a new member, I am working hard to prepare for the 2014 ACM-ICPC competition with the Cornell ACM Programming Team.

Autonomous Biped Research

I am a researcher for Prof. Ruina's robotics and biomechanics lab. I am currently working on simulations and high-level coding of the Cornell Autonomous Biped.
